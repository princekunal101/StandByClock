Privacy Policy — Standby Clock

Effective Date: Jan, 2026  |  Last Updated: Jan, 2026

1. Introduction

Standby Clock provides a dynamic, always-on clock interface that remains visible even when your phone is locked. We respect your privacy and aim to be transparent about how the app operates and what information it uses.

2. Information we collect

Calendar date and time: The app reads the current system date and time from your device solely to display accurate clock information.

No personal data: The app does not collect, store, or share personal information, contacts, messages, files, or identifiers.

3. Permissions

Lock-screen display: The app may request permission to show on the lock screen so the clock remains visible when your device is locked.

No other permissions: The app does not request access to location, camera, microphone, storage, or other sensitive permissions.

4. Notifications

No notifications: The app does not send push notifications, alerts, or background messages at this time.

5. Usage recommendations

Persistent screen-on: Because the app can keep your screen active for extended periods, we recommend connecting your device to a charger during long sessions to reduce battery drain.

6. Data sharing

No third-party sharing: The app does not share data with third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or external services.

7. Security

The app runs locally on your device. No data is transmitted externally, helping protect your privacy and security.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected in app update notes and on this page.
